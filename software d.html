Process

1) Research and Formalize essential processes into usage scenarios, diagrams and schemes;.

2) Offer and describe technical solution options that meet all requirements.

3) Create models demonstrating appropriateness of desired solutions.

4) Estimate costs and timelines of implementation and suggest ways of optimization.

5) Carry out a thorough review and analysis of the source code, documentation, executable modules and change history using reverse engineering methods.

6) As an outcome create technical requirements, high and low level architecture description and other technical and end-user documentation.

7) Conduct suitable system audit and gap analysis.

8) Suggest and describe available options for resolution of the discovered issues/

9) Analyze the requirements and present the technical part of a tendering procedure in the most unambiguous way so there is complete clarity.

10) Analyze existing development processes, their formalization and maturity level, correspondence to the CMMI standards and/or Lean/Agile principles.

11) Suggest steps for improvement in accordance with your business direction.
